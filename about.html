About Sam's Goods

Empowering culture through authentic creative services

Our Story

Sam's Goods, LLC is a Sacramento-based creative enterprise founded by Sam Jones with a mission to empower culture through expressive, high-quality, and authentic creative services.

What started as a passion for creative expression has evolved into a comprehensive multimedia company offering custom apparel, sticker printing, graphic design, music production, and video content creation.

After securing business credit lines and establishing strong industry connections, Sam's Goods is positioned to scale operations and offer bundled multimedia solutions for artists, businesses, and creative individuals who demand excellence.

We believe that every brand, every artist, and every creative vision deserves professional-quality execution. That's why we've built our services around collaboration, quality, and authentic creative expression.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, authentic creative services that empower individuals and businesses to express their unique vision and connect with their audience.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We use premium materials and professional-grade equipment
  • Authentic Expression: Every project reflects the client's unique vision
  • Collaborative Process: We work closely with clients throughout the creative journey
  • Fair Pricing: Professional services at accessible rates
  • Community Focus: Supporting local artists and businesses in Sacramento and beyond

Meet the Founder

Sam Jones

Founder & Creative Director

Sam Jones is a multi-disciplinary creative professional with expertise spanning music production, graphic design, video creation, and custom apparel. Based in Sacramento, CA, Sam founded Sam's Goods, LLC to provide comprehensive creative services under one roof.

With a background in both the technical and artistic aspects of creative production, Sam brings a unique perspective to every project. Whether it's producing a beat, designing a logo, or creating a music video, Sam's approach is always collaborative and focused on bringing the client's vision to life.

Sam's commitment to quality and authentic expression has made Sam's Goods a trusted partner for artists, businesses, and individuals looking to elevate their brand and creative presence.
