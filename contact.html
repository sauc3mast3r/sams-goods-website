Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a custom quote?

Use our booking form or send us an email with your project details. We'll provide a detailed quote within 24 hours.

What's your typical turnaround time?

Most projects are completed within 1-2 weeks. Rush orders are available for an additional fee. Timeline depends on project complexity and current workload.

Do you work with clients outside Sacramento?

Yes! We work with clients nationwide. Many services can be completed remotely, and we can ship physical products anywhere in the US.

What's included in the deposit?

A 50% deposit is required to begin work. This secures your spot in our schedule and covers initial project setup. The deposit is non-refundable if canceled within 48 hours of the scheduled start.

How many revisions are included?

All services include up to 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are billed at $25/hour to ensure fair pricing for all clients.

Can I see examples of your work?

Follow us on Instagram @samosupreme2025 for our latest work, or request a portfolio during your consultation.
