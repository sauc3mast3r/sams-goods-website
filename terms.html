Beat Licensing Terms

This Music Licensing Agreement governs the use of beats and music produced by Sam's Goods, LLC.

1. Grant of License

Sam's Goods, LLC ("Licensor") grants the purchaser ("Licensee") rights to use the specified beat for recording, performance, and distribution as outlined below.

2. License Types

Beat Leasing (Non-Exclusive)
  • Distribution up to 50,000 streams or copies
  • Non-exclusive rights (beat may be licensed to others)
  • Price range: $30-$75
  • Upgrade to exclusive required once limit reached
Exclusive Beat Purchase
  • Full exclusive rights to the beat
  • Unlimited distribution and usage
  • Price range: $150-$400
  • Beat removed from marketplace after purchase

3. Usage Requirements

  • Credit required: "Prod. by Sam's Goods" on all releases

  • Commercial use permitted within license terms

4. Payment & Ownership

  • License fee payable in full upon agreement
  • No additional royalties owed unless specified
  • Licensor retains copyright ownership (lease agreements)
  • Full ownership transferred (exclusive purchases only)
